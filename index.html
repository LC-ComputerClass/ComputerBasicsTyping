Language: Spanish

About The Workshop

Welcome to the "Computer Basics & Typing" workshop! This session is designed to help you develop a solid understanding of fundamental computer concepts and improve your typing skills. Whether you are a complete beginner or looking to refresh your knowledge, this workshop will provide you with the essential tools to navigate the digital world with ease.

What You'll Learn

In this workshop, you will:

  • Understand the Basics of Computers:

    Learn what a computer is, how it works, and the key components that make up a computer.

  • Explore How Computers Operate:

    Discover the relationship between hardware and software, and understand how they work together to perform tasks.

  • Understand the Operating System:

    Gain knowledge about the operating system, including its role in managing your computer's resources and helping you interact with your device.

  • Identify Different Types of Computers:

    Learn about various types of computers from desktops to servers and mobile devices, and their specific uses in different contexts.

  • Master the Use of the Mouse:

    Improve your skills with the mouse, including how to click, double-click, right-click, and scroll, for efficient interaction with your computer.

  • Become Proficient with the Keyboard:

    Understand the layout of the keyboard and how to use it to type effectively.

  • Utilize Time-Saving Keyboard Shortcuts:

    Learn essential shortcuts to make tasks such as copying, pasting, and undoing quicker and more efficient.

Prerequisites

This workshop is designed for beginners, so no prior experience with computers or typing is necessary. Computers will be provided to all workshop attendees, ensuring you have the tools needed to participate fully. While familiarity with using a mouse and keyboard can be helpful, the most important requirement is a willingness to learn and practice the basic computer skills covered during the session.

Whether you are new to computers or looking to improve your typing abilities, this workshop offers a supportive, hands-on environment to build confidence and enhance your skills.
